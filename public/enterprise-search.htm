Questions & Answers

How does Notion AI search across multiple apps?

Using AI connectors, Notion AI performs internal knowledge search by indexing content from your work tools based on your permissions. When you ask a question, it searches Slack messages, files and PDFs in Google Drive, Jira tickets, GitHub Issues & Pull Requests, and other connected apps to deliver the most relevant information—fast.

Is Notion AI secure?

Yes. Notion AI only shows information you have permission to access. It follows strict security standards like SOC 2 Type 2, ISO 27001, GDPR, and CCPA compliance, with zero data retention for enterprise users.

Can Notion AI create reports from multiple sources?

Absolutely. With Research Mode, Notion AI analyzes data from your connected apps and the web to generate detailed, ready-to-share reports—saving teams days of manual research.

Which tools can I connect to Notion AI?

You can integrate apps like Slack, Google Drive, GitHub, Jira, MSFT Teams, SharePoint, OneDrive today with others coming soon: Linear, Gmail, Salesforce, Zendesk, Box and more. These integrations enable a true unified search experience across all your internal and external knowledge sources.

Can I choose which sources Notion AI searches?

Yes. You control where and how Notion AI searches by selecting which apps, documents, and web sources to include—giving you full control over your search experience.

How is Notion AI different from traditional enterprise search tools?

Unlike traditional search, Notion AI doesn't just find files—it understands questions, retrieves up-to-date answers with citations, and even uses critical thinking to analyze complex questions and generate detailed research reports in minutes.
